A bit about Mark

I’m not sure how many people my age would agree with me, but being born in the 1990s and living into the 21st century, I feel pretty lucky. I love being able to learn about and use the technology we have access to daily. I think everyone is aware that there are cons to having technology as well, such as: fake news on social media, cellphones getting robocalls, and procrastination due to Netflix. However, never in human history have we had the ability to do such things as: share images with multiple people around the world in seconds, search for our next homes online, or use our phones to avoid traffic jams. This is up for debate, but I think the pros of technology outweigh the cons. My life plans are to try and use the time and place we live in to, hopefully, improve the lives of those I can.
