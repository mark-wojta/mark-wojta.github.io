A bit about Mark's plans

I’m not sure how many people my age would agree with me, but being born in the 1990s and living into the 21st century, I feel pretty lucky. I love being able to learn about and use the technology we have access to daily. I think everyone is aware that there are cons to having technology as well, such as: fake news on social media, cellphones getting robocalls, and procrastination due to Netflix. However, never in human history have we had the ability to do such things as: share images with multiple people around the world in seconds, search for our next homes online, or use our phones to avoid traffic jams. This is up for debate, but I think the pros of technology outweigh the cons. My intentions are to take advantage of the time and place I live in. With the help of technology, I plan on sustaining and/or improving the lives of those I can impact, now and into the future.
